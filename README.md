My name is Jennifer Noinaj (noy-NAH), and I go by Jenn. I'm located in Chicago! I'm currently a Service Designer at FSA focused on improving the borrower experience for our loan forgiveness products. I spend my days navigating the intersection of product, design, and strategy.

🔍 **What I’m doing at FSA**
* **End-to-End Design:** Bridging the gap between user needs and policy through journey mapping and service blueprinting.
* **Evidence-Based Decisions:** Blending qualitative user interviews with product analytics to drive design strategy.
* **Systems Thinking:** Designing the "behind-the-scenes" processes that make the front-end experience seamless for borrowers.

🚀 **Beyond the Pixels**
I’m big on shaping the culture behind the work and a firm believer that healthy teams make better products. I help teams evolve from the 'waterfall' mindset to agile, cross-functional collaboration. Whether it's upskilling staff or maturing design practices, I love building the "machine that builds the product." 

🛠 **My Toolkit**
* **Research:** User Interviews, Data Analytics, Usability Testing
* **Strategy:** Service Blueprinting, Journey Mapping, Ecosystem Mapping
* **Execution:** Product Design, Agile Methodology, Cross-functional Leadership

📥 **How I Work**
* **Default to Transparency:** I share early and often.
* **Research First:** If we aren't talking to users, we're guessing.
* **Collaborative by Design:** I believe the best ideas come from the "silos" we're trying to break down.
